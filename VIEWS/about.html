Project Overview

Smart Harvest is an IoT-enabled monitoring system with AI predictive analytics designed to optimize Azolla farming. Our platform provides real-time monitoring, predictive analysis, and actionable insights to help farmers achieve optimal growth conditions and maximize yields.
